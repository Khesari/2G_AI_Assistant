Support Services in India

Domestic Violence Reporting

If you or someone you know is a victim of domestic violence in India, you can report it to the nearest police station or contact helplines like 181 Women Helpline or 1091.

Mental Health Counseling

Mental health support is essential. Reach out to organizations like Vandrevala Foundation or Roshni for counseling and assistance.

Career Guidance Support

For career guidance and support, consider visiting the National Career Service (NCS) website or contacting local career counseling centers.

Emergency Contacts in India

In case of emergencies, call 112 for all emergency services in India. You can also find specific emergency contact numbers for police, fire, and medical assistance in your state.
